YogiRef is an app to help yoga students and teachers learn and reference poses. YogiRef has two modes, list and flash card. In list mode poses can be quickly referenced via search or simple scrolling. In flash card mode the poses are displayed in a random order.

In both list and flash card modes the cards include: a picture of the pose, the English name, the Sanskrit name, the Sanskrit pronunciation (spelled out), an audio file of the Sanskrit, and the meaning of each Sanskrit word. You can also now makes your own decks which can be viewed in either mode! The app includes 89 poses
